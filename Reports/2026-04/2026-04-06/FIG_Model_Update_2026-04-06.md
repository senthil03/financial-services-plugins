## 🚨 The "Broken IPO" Thesis
Figma’s 2025 IPO was met with technical selling following the blocked Adobe merger. Currently trading at **$20.00**, the company has shed 50% of its peak private valuation.

- **REVENUE GROWTH**: Reporting a consistent **35% YoY** revenue trajectory, driven by the rollout of **FigJam Enterprise** and specialized **Figma Dev Mode** licenses.
- **PROFITABILITY MIRAGE**: Figma remains Net-Loss negative but is **Free Cash Flow Neutral** (Rule of 40 score: 35). This suggests high operational efficiency compared to peers like SNOW or SHOP.
- **THE AI THREAT**: The market is penalizing FIG due to **"Generative Design"** rumors (e.g., Adobe Firefly, Canva Magic Studio). However, Figma's current integration of **Gen-AI for Design-Ops** (auto-layout, layer naming, prototype linking) is increasing seat-stickiness, not reducing seat-count.
