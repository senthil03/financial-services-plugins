## Strategic Outlook & Alpha Thesis
SoundHound AI is currently in a "show me" phase. While revenue growth (52%) and organic automotive growth (88%) are exceptional, the $300M ATM offering has reset the valuation multiple.

**The Bear Case:** Profitability remains elusive. Gross margin contraction (31% vs 36% YoY) is a red flag if it persists into Q2. The dilution from the ATM offering may weigh on the share price until the LivePerson deal is fully digested and synergistic revenue is proven.
