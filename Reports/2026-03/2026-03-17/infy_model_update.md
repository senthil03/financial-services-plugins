## Step 5: Reconciling the "Hold" Rating (Counter-Thesis)

The market currently reflects a consensus **"Hold"** (5/12 analysts) with bear-case concerns centered on:
1.  **Technical Weakness**: Stock hitting 52-week lows in March 2026.
2.  **Currency Risk**: 13% INR depreciation over 3 years impacting ADS holders.
3.  **AI Disruption**: Fears that GenAI will cannibalize heritage labor-arbitrage revenue.

**Why we disagree (The "Buy" Case):**
- **Deal Quality**: The $4.8B Q3 deal win (57% net new) is a leading indicator of revenue acceleration that technical backward-looking charts miss.
- **Margin Stability**: Adjusted margins of 21.2% remain robust; the 12.3% attrition rate (industry low) provides significant operational stability.
- **AI as a Tailwind**: Through the Topaz platform, AI is contributing 5.5% to revenue and growing—representing a shift toward high-margin consulting rather than just cannibalization.
