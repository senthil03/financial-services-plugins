## 🧠 Our Take: The Contrarian Opportunity
**Market view: "Hold/Sell"** — Fear of AI disruption and technical breakdowns are driving the current price action.

**Antigravity view: "BUY"** — We maintain our conviction for three reasons:
1.  **Fundamental Divergence**: While the price is hitting lows, the **order book is hitting highs** ($4.8B large deals last quarter).
2.  **AI is an Enabler, not just a Killer**: INFY’s Topaz platform is already contributing 5.5% to revenue. Nvidia's growth actually drives demand for the *integration* services INFY provides.
3.  **Valuation Anchor**: Trading at a multi-year trough multiple despite raising FY26 revenue guidance to 3.0%–3.5%.
